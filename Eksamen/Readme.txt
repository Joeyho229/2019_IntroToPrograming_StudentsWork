This is a simple piano with a few of the many cords. This is by no mean to be musically correct.

User guide:
The greyed out letters are shortkeys to operate the piano.
!!! The according letters will no be activated if caps lock is on !!!

Additionally its also possible to activate the according sounds with mouse clicks.

There is also a simple counter up in the cornor, counting how many notes has been played.
